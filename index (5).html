Welcome to Bloom

Inspiring Future Leaders in Ras Al Khaimah

At Bloom American International School, we are committed to delivering an outstanding American curriculum education aligned with international standards. We believe every child has the potential to thrive, lead, and make a meaningful impact on the world.

Our mission is to nurture students' wellbeing, character, and personal growth while embedding UAE values, culture, and national identity — creating a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment that prepares every student for university success and the careers of tomorrow.

"At Bloom, we nurture curiosity, inspire excellence, and empower students to thrive in an ever-changing world."

Academic Programmes

Your Child's Complete Learning Journey

KG1 – KG2

Early Years Foundation

A joyful and nurturing environment where children learn through play, exploration, and hands-on experiences. Focused on the whole child — building communication, creativity, and a genuine love of learning from day one.

Children develop confidence, independence, and foundational skills in literacy and numeracy through carefully designed, child-led activities supported by specialist teachers.

Early Literacy & NumeracyCommunication & LanguageCreativity & ImaginationSocial & Emotional GrowthInquiry-Based LearningPhysical Development
🌱
Grades 1 – 5

Elementary School

Students build strong academic foundations while developing curiosity, confidence, and independence. Specialist educators deliver engaging, student-centred lessons across all core subjects and enrichment areas.

The curriculum balances rigorous core academics with arts, sports, technology, and innovation — ensuring every child discovers their strengths and passions.

English Language ArtsMathematicsScienceSocial StudiesArabic & Islamic EducationTechnology & InnovationArts & PE
📖
Grades 6 – 8

Middle School

Students are challenged academically while developing leadership, responsibility, and critical thinking. STEM innovation projects, collaboration, and personal growth programmes prepare students for the demands of high school.

Middle school is a key transitional phase — we recognise this and provide strong pastoral care alongside ambitious academic goals.

Advanced Academic SkillsSTEM & InnovationStudent LeadershipCollaborationWellbeing & Pastoral Care
🔬
Grades 9 – 12

High School

Rigorous academics, leadership opportunities, and personalised pathways prepare students for the world's best universities. University counselling, advanced electives, entrepreneurship, and community service shape globally aware graduates.

Our dedicated college counsellors guide students through every stage of the university application process, from shortlisting to acceptance.

College & Career ReadinessAdvanced ElectivesUniversity CounsellingEntrepreneurshipCommunity ServiceGlobal Awareness
